# The Story Behind ARAKKHA

In February 2026, I was an HR Manager at a factory in Rayong, Thailand.
No computer science degree. No AI background. Just curiosity.

I started asking: what if I could build an AI that thinks like me?
Not a generic assistant. A digital twin. My personality. My voice. My way of thinking.

6 weeks later:
- Fennix is running 24/7 on a server
- ARAKKHA v4 is trained on 14,270 pairs of my real conversations
- The architecture I designed independently aligns with where serious AI research is heading

Built after work hours. RTX 4060 laptop. Rayong, Thailand. 🇹🇭
